About the Artist

Dreamer | Multitasker | Experimenter | Artist

I'm known as Starlitzart but it's just a term for Starlitza's art. Just call me Star for short. Starlitza is my character's name , which you see on display. She's a representation of myself in a different world. I have a passion for design, creativity and constant motivation to be a better artist. My main inspiration is the stars. I don't know why I became so infatuated with space, but it just happened. I became an artist who likes to bring joy through my art, experiment different medias, and express creativity. I used to be a hobbyist and doodle every other day, but starting 2018 I began to get into art more and started practicing on my own. I don't own any fancy tablets or software. Instead, I use my phone and used an app called Ibispaint X as my go to software. I tend to explore on my own first, and see what I am capable of. I rarely use tutorials unless I need new inpiration or practice. What you see here on my website are art/commission examples I made on my phone which is modified in Photoshop for sizing.
Thank you for stopping by!
